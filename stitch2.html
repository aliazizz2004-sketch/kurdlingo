ephemeral

/əˈfem(ə)rəl/

adj.
1.

Lasting for a very short time.

"The sunset provided an ephemeral glow across the mountain range."

2.

(Chiefly of plants) having a very short life cycle.

"Ephemeral desert flowers bloom only after the rare spring rains."
